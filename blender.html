Vitamix Ascent X5 Blender

The Vitamix Ascent X5 is Good Housekeeping’s best‑overall blender for 2025. This full‑size powerhouse offers intuitive controls, versatile presets and excellent blending performance, replacing the previous A3500 model.

Key Features

  • Offers ten different preset programs and built‑in technology that alerts you when to use the tamper; these features contributed to outstanding performance in lab tests【656971337530402†L189-L200】.
  • Weighs 13.5 pounds with dimensions of 7.75 × 9.75 × 17 inches and a 48‑ounce jar; the control panel includes variable speed control, a timer, pulse and presets for smoothies, frozen desserts, hot soups, frozen cocktails, dips, smoothie bowls, frappes, nut butter, non‑dairy milk and spice grinding【656971337530402†L199-L203】.
  • Features a modern touch interface, programmable timer, variable speed and ten preset settings; technology on the digital display reminds you to use the tamper during thick blends and warns when the blender overheats【656971337530402†L205-L212】.
  • Lab tests showed it producing creamy smoothies, silky margaritas, frothy milkshakes and smooth pureed soup; peanut butter came out slightly chunkier but can be smoothed with a bit of oil【656971337530402†L214-L219】.

Why It’s Great

Versatility is the Vitamix Ascent X5’s biggest strength. It handles everything from smoothies and frozen cocktails to nut butters and hot soups with ease. The intuitive controls and built‑in reminders take the guesswork out of blending, and the included tamper helps you achieve perfect consistency every time.

Considerations

This blender is heavier and more expensive than many competitors. At around $700, it’s a serious investment and its 13.5‑pound weight may be unwieldy for some users. Some testers also noted that without added oil, peanut butter can be chunkier than desired【656971337530402†L214-L219】.
